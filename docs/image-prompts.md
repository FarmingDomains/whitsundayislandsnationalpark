## Additional generation prompts (15–20)

1. **Hero late afternoon Whitehaven** — Long empty silica beach, soft golden side-light, turquoise shallows, hoop pines silhouette, no people.  
2. **Hill Inlet mid-high tide classic swirl** — Elevated lookout POV, geometric sand channels, bright tropical sun.  
3. **Hill Inlet low-tide contrast** — Broad sand flats, muted channels (education: tides change the look).  
4. **Tongue Point landing** — Tender/water taxi at rocky-sand landing, day packs, practical logistics mood.  
5. **Solway Passage multi-island view** — From track or headland, deep channels between islands.  
6. **Dugong Beach quiet camp** — Two tents, dry bags, stove (no fire), empty dawn beach.  
7. **Chance Bay seclusion** — Narrow beach, rainforest edge, overcast soft light.  
8. **Ngaro Cultural Site approach (respectful)** — Signed boardwalk/path, interpretive panels unreadable, no rock art close-ups, no people in ceremony.  
9. **Airlie Beach marina departure** — Early morning vessels, practical hub energy, no brand logos.  
10. **Shute Harbour coach/boat connection** — Transfer context, travel photography.  
11. **Fringing reef surface snorkel** — Mask-level waterline, coral heads, ethical distance.  
12. **Butterfly Bay calm anchorage** — Yacht at public mooring context (generic, no branding).  
13. **Overnight stars on Whitehaven** — Tent silhouette, Milky Way, no light pollution fantasy overload.  
14. **Wet-season moody Whitsunday** — Heavy cloud, slate water, dramatic but safe (no storm heroics).  
15. **Dry-season clear panorama** — Peak blue sky, multiple islands, postcard clarity.  
16. **Turtle at respectful distance** — Surface glimpse from boat, no chasing/handling.  
17. **Whale season distant spout** — Far offshore, winter light (optional seasonal).  
18. **Hill Inlet track steps** — Closed-toe shoes POV, moderate effort realism.  
19. **Packing flat-lay tropical boat kit** — Stinger suit, dry bag, reef-safe sunscreen, water jugs (product-neutral).  
20. **Family day-tour beach time** — Soft focus, non-identifiable backs to camera, flotation vests.
